Our Vision

"Only when you empathise with those you impact will the process of ideation yield radical results"

We at inSight work towards making the corporate workforce more inclusive, creating a culture of diversity which holds merit as a competitive differentiator - whether it be the inclusion of race, ethnicity, or ability.We strive towards cultivating an inclusive culture created by a supportive infrastructure. Creating an accessible workplace for people of all abilities to be able to function productively.

inSight is a step towards political, social, cultural, and educational reform. Owing to the history of oppression, marginalization and exclusion faced by transgenders, LGBT individuals, women and disabled individuals, the social community has never been open to their voices but we give a voice to their ideas. Our platform is built on a foundation of love and empathy which helps draw connections between individuals based on their experiences in their community and therefore contribute to constructive relationships in everyday life.

We hope to create a sustainable student-run organization that works towards creating an equality of opportunity for marginalized communities.
